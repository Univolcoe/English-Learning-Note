## 冠词
- 特指 the （定冠词）
- a / an

### 比较级
|构成方法|原型|比较级|最高级|
|:--:|:--:|:--:|:--:|
|+er|cool|cooler|coolest|
|以字幕e结尾-r|	|	|	|
|重读闭音节，双写结尾辅音字母+er|	|	|	|
|辅音+y : 变y为i加er|busy|buisier|buisiest|
两个以上的音节一定加more | most.
for instance:
> He is taller than me.
> He is taller than he last year.
> The city is buisier than it was in the past.
> The city is buisier than it used to be in the past.
